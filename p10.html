HIV/AIDS Diagnoses in NYC

This is a data visualization of HIV/AIDS diagnoses in all five boroughs of New York City, broken down by race and location. The data comes from NYC Open Data.

Designing the Data

What a hellish process this was. Loading the JSON set challenged me a lot more than I anticipated. Ultimately, it came down to understanding arrays. I had to take the data and push everything into its own array so I was able to pull from each specific subset in order to best display HIV and AIDS diagnoses.
